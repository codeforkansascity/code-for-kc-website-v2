We are
code for Kansas City

We’re Kansas City’s chapter of the Code for America Brigade network, a group of civic-minded geeks who volunteer our talents to projects that improve how our local governments and community organizations use the web.

We strive for principles of open data and technology advocacy for civic good and are supported by our host organization, KC Digital Drive. The Brigade is about action, so join us at a Hack Night where we’ll commit code to make a difference in our city!
